ABOUT

Hello! My name is Taylor Emolo. I am a front-end web developer & designer. For as long as I can remember, I've always had a strong interest and appreciation for art and design. Now that I am embarking on my new career path as a web developer, I focus on the aesthetic details of a website while ensuring it's functional. I am currently attending Rutger Coding Bootcamp which is An intensive 24-week long boot camp dedicated to designing and building web applications. I am well-versed in programming languages and applications, including JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, HTML5/CSS3, Bootstrap, Git, Responsive Design, API/JSON, RESTful API, Firebase, MySQL, React, Meteor, State Management, and Command Line.


I graduated from Pace University with a degree in Business Administration, in addition to taking supplemental graduate courses at the Fashion Institute of Technology.
